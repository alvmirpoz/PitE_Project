Our predictions are based on predictive models trained on 6700 cases.
We have trained a total of 6 different models, using 3 algorithms and an empirical optimization of their hyperparameters.

Here we can see a graph generated with a test of 3300 test cases. Comparing the models according to the Binary Cross Entropy obtained, we can have an approximate idea of how reliable they have been (this measure is a 'loss', so the closer to 0, the more reliable the model).

As we can see, the best model has turned out to be the one using the Decision Tree algorithm, with tree depth hyperparameter equal to 3.
